fdrd the tiny feed reader

Here you can put a URL pointing to an OPML file, and then all posts from the feeds mentioned in that OPML file will get fetched.

Note that while all domains with support for CORS will be fetched directly, the ones lacking support will get fetched via a CORS proxy. Beware of the risk associated with piping your traffic through another server. If you want to avoid this, remove the proxy URL from the form below, but beware that this will error on a lot of feeds.
